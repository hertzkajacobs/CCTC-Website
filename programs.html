Adult Program

Comprehensive Addiction Treatment for Adults

Our adult program provides a full continuum of care — meeting you where you are and walking with you every step of the way toward lasting sobriety.

Our adult treatment program is designed for individuals 18 and older who are struggling with alcohol or drug addiction. We offer a structured, supportive environment where every care plan is individualized to address your unique history, needs, and goals.

From the moment you arrive, our multidisciplinary team of licensed clinicians, medical professionals, and recovery coaches works alongside you to build a pathway to sustained recovery.

Medical Detox

24/7 medically supervised withdrawal management to safely cleanse your body and prepare you for treatment.

Residential Inpatient (30/60/90-Day)

Structured 24/7 care in a safe, therapeutic environment with daily individual and group therapy sessions.

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

Intensive day treatment — 5 days a week — as you begin transitioning back to daily life.

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

Flexible group and individual therapy sessions designed to fit around work and family commitments.

Outpatient Counseling

Regular one-on-one and group sessions to reinforce coping skills and maintain long-term sobriety.

Aftercare & Alumni Support

Ongoing relapse prevention planning, peer support groups, and alumni connections to keep you strong.

Adolescent Program • Ages 13–18

Specialized Treatment & Education for Teens

A structured, age-appropriate program that combines clinical addiction treatment with continued academic support — so teens can heal without falling behind.

Our adolescent program serves young people ages 13 to 18 who are struggling with substance use disorder. We understand that teens have unique clinical, developmental, and educational needs — which is why our program brings together an integrated team of addiction clinicians and educational staff working side by side.

Treatment takes place in a safe, structured setting separate from adults, with programming specifically designed for adolescent development, peer dynamics, and family healing.

Clinical & Educational Team

Our on-site educational team ensures teens keep up with schoolwork throughout treatment, partnering with their home school district as needed.

Structured Inpatient Program

A fully supervised residential setting with a daily schedule of therapy, education, recreation, and life-skills groups.

Group Therapy Sessions

Peer-based group sessions led by licensed adolescent therapists, focusing on communication, healthy relationships, and relapse prevention.

Individual Therapy Sessions

One-on-one sessions with a dedicated adolescent counselor addressing underlying trauma, mental health, and personal recovery goals.

Full Continuum of Care

Residential inpatient, Partial Hospitalization (PHP), Intensive Outpatient (IOP), and standard outpatient — the same proven steps, tailored for teens.

Family Involvement

Family therapy sessions and parent education workshops are a core part of every adolescent treatment plan.
