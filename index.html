About Us

We Are More Than A Treatment Facility — We Are A Community Dedicated To Restoring Lives.

At Cherry Creek Treatment Center, we provide comprehensive, evidence-based care for individuals struggling with alcohol and drug addiction. Our multidisciplinary team of licensed clinicians, medical professionals, and recovery coaches delivers personalized treatment that addresses the whole person — mind, body, and spirit.

From medically supervised detox through residential care, PHP, and ongoing outpatient support, we offer a full continuum of care designed to meet you wherever you are in your recovery journey.

Our Services

Comprehensive Care at Every Stage of Recovery

We offer a full continuum of addiction treatment — from medical detox to long-term aftercare — so you're supported every step of the way.

Medical Detox

Safe, medically supervised withdrawal management to cleanse your body from alcohol or drugs while keeping you comfortable and stable.

Learn More

Residential Treatment

Immersive 24/7 inpatient care in a structured, supportive environment — available in 30, 60, and 90-day programs tailored to your needs.

Learn More

Partial Hospitalization (PHP)

Intensive day treatment — typically 5 days a week — providing clinical structure while you begin transitioning back to daily life.

Learn More

Intensive Outpatient (IOP)

Flexible group and individual therapy sessions — 3 to 5 days per week — that fit around work, school, and family commitments.

Learn More

Outpatient Program

Regular counseling and support sessions designed to maintain sobriety and build coping skills while living at home.

Learn More

Aftercare & Alumni

Ongoing relapse prevention, peer support groups, and alumni connections to keep you strong and connected to your recovery community.

Learn More
Our Programs

Separate Programs for Adults & Adolescents

We offer two distinct, age-appropriate treatment tracks — each with its own specialized clinical team — built on the same evidence-based continuum of care.

Adult Program

Adult Program

Comprehensive addiction treatment for adults 18 and older, covering the full continuum from medical detox and residential inpatient through PHP, IOP, outpatient, and long-term aftercare support.

  • Medical Detox & Residential Inpatient
  • Partial Hospitalization (PHP) & IOP
  • Individual & Group Therapy
  • Dual Diagnosis & Co-occurring Disorders
  • Aftercare & Alumni Support

Adolescent Program Ages 13 – 18

Structured inpatient and outpatient care designed specifically for teens, integrating clinical addiction treatment with an on-site educational team so adolescents heal without falling behind in school.

  • Structured Inpatient Program
  • Clinical & On-Site Educational Team
  • Group & Individual Therapy Sessions
  • PHP, IOP & Outpatient Levels of Care
  • Family Therapy & Parent Education
