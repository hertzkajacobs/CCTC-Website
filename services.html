Medical Detox

Safe, Medically Supervised Detoxification

Medical detox is the critical first step for many individuals entering recovery. Attempting to withdraw from alcohol, opioids, benzodiazepines, or other substances without medical supervision can be dangerous — even life-threatening.

At Cherry Creek Treatment Center, our board-certified physicians and licensed nursing staff monitor your withdrawal around the clock, using FDA-approved medications to manage symptoms and keep you as comfortable and safe as possible.

  • 24/7 medical monitoring by licensed clinicians
  • Medication-assisted treatment (MAT) when appropriate
  • Alcohol, opioid, benzo, stimulant & poly-substance detox
  • Seamless transition into residential or PHP
  • Dual diagnosis assessment during detox
  • Compassionate, judgment-free environment

Residential Treatment

Immersive Inpatient Residential Care

Our residential program provides a full-immersion recovery experience in a safe, structured, and supportive environment. Removing yourself from the triggers of daily life allows you to focus entirely on healing.

Clients participate in individual therapy, group counseling, family sessions, holistic wellness activities, and evidence-based modalities including CBT, DBT, and motivational interviewing — all within a structured daily schedule designed to rebuild healthy habits.

  • 30, 60, and 90-day program options
  • Individual & group therapy daily
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)
  • Family therapy & visitation program
  • Holistic therapies: yoga, mindfulness, art
  • Nutritional support & fitness programming
  • 12-step and non-12-step tracks available

Partial Hospitalization Program

Intensive Day Treatment — PHP

Our Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP) offers the clinical intensity of inpatient treatment while allowing clients to return home — or to a sober living environment — in the evenings. PHP is ideal as a step-down from residential care or as a primary treatment level for those with a stable home environment.

Clients attend programming 5–6 hours per day, 5 days a week, engaging in group therapy, individual sessions, psychiatric services, and skill-building workshops.

  • 5–6 hours of structured programming per day
  • Psychiatric evaluation & medication management
  • Process groups, psychoeducation & skills groups
  • Individual therapy sessions weekly
  • Case management & discharge planning
  • Ideal step-down from residential treatment

Intensive Outpatient Program

Flexible, Structured Outpatient Treatment — IOP

Our Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP) is designed for individuals who need structured treatment but also need flexibility to maintain work, school, or family responsibilities. IOP provides more support than standard outpatient while keeping you connected to your everyday life.

Sessions meet 3–4 days per week for 3 hours per session, combining evidence-based group therapy, relapse prevention training, and individual counseling. Morning and evening session options are available.

  • 9–12 hours of treatment per week
  • Morning & evening scheduling options
  • Relapse prevention & coping skills training
  • Peer support & group therapy
  • Dual diagnosis co-occurring disorder treatment
  • Ideal for working adults and students
Learn About IOP
Outpatient Program

Ongoing Outpatient Counseling & Support

Our standard Outpatient Program (OP) provides continued clinical support for individuals who have completed a higher level of care or for those whose addiction severity allows for less intensive treatment while maintaining their daily routines.

Weekly individual therapy, group sessions, and medication management services give clients the tools and accountability needed to maintain long-term sobriety.

  • 1–2 sessions per week
  • Individual therapy with a licensed counselor
  • Group counseling & peer accountability
  • Medication-assisted treatment support
  • Continued family therapy as needed
  • Relapse intervention & step-up support

Aftercare & Alumni

Lifelong Support After Treatment

Recovery doesn't end when formal treatment does. Our Aftercare and Alumni program provides the ongoing connection, accountability, and resources needed to thrive long after leaving Cherry Creek.

We stay with our alumni through regular check-ins, peer support groups, community events, and relapse prevention planning — ensuring you always have a community to lean on during the challenges of daily life in recovery.

  • Personalized aftercare planning at discharge
  • Weekly alumni support group meetings
  • Recovery coaching & sober mentor matching
  • Sober living referrals & housing assistance
  • Alumni events & community activities
  • 24/7 crisis intervention line for alumni
