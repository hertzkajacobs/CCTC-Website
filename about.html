Our Story

Founded On Compassion, Built On Results

Cherry Creek Treatment Center was founded with a single, unwavering belief: every person struggling with addiction deserves access to compassionate, high-quality care — no matter their background or circumstances.

Over more than 15 years, we have grown from a small residential facility into a comprehensive treatment center offering a full continuum of addiction care. Through each stage of our growth, our core values have remained the same: dignity, evidence-based practice, and genuine human connection.

We have helped hundreds of individuals and families find their way through addiction and into lasting recovery. Our alumni are our greatest testament — people who rebuilt careers, mended relationships, and discovered new purpose in their lives.

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Everything We Do

Our values are not just words on a wall — they are the foundation of every interaction, every treatment plan, and every decision we make on behalf of our patients.

Compassion First

We meet every patient with empathy and dignity, free of judgment. Addiction is a disease, and everyone who comes through our doors deserves to be treated with respect and care.

Evidence-Based Care

Every treatment modality we use is grounded in clinical research and proven best practices. We continuously update our programs to reflect advances in addiction medicine.

Individualized Treatment

No two people are the same, and neither are their paths to recovery. Every care plan at Cherry Creek is uniquely tailored to the individual's needs, history, and goals.

Family Involvement

Addiction affects the entire family. We integrate family therapy, education, and support into our programs to help heal relationships and build a strong network for lasting recovery.

Lasting Recovery

Our goal isn't just sobriety — it's a life worth living. We equip our patients with the skills, tools, and community they need to thrive long after leaving our center.

Privacy & Confidentiality

We uphold the strictest standards of patient confidentiality. Your privacy is sacred — we are fully HIPAA-compliant and treat all personal information with absolute discretion.

Our Approach

Treating the Whole Person, Not Just the Addiction

We understand that addiction rarely exists in isolation. Most individuals entering treatment are also dealing with co-occurring mental health conditions such as depression, anxiety, trauma, or PTSD — often referred to as dual diagnosis.

Our integrated treatment model addresses the physical, psychological, emotional, and spiritual dimensions of addiction simultaneously. This whole-person approach leads to deeper healing and more durable recovery outcomes.

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)
Trauma-Informed Care & EMDR
